Full description of the project...

We designed a comprehensive mobile healthcare application for MedCare Solutions with a strong focus on patient experience and accessibility. The app helps patients manage appointments, medications, and communicate with healthcare providers.

## Key Features

- Appointment scheduling
- Medication reminders
- Secure messaging with doctors
- Health records access
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliant

## Design Principles

- Patient-first approach
- Clear information hierarchy
- Accessible for all users
- Privacy and security
